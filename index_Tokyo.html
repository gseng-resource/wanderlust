Optimized Shopping Routes

Starting/Ending Point: Hotel Gracery Shinjuku Tokyo (1-19-1 Kabukicho, Shinjuku City, Tokyo 160-0021, Japan)

Route A: Watch Focus

This route focuses on vintage Rolex watches, starting with shops in Shinjuku and then moving to Nakano.

Top 5 Shops:

  • Komehyo Shinjuku Store
  • Daikokuya Shinjuku Main Store
  • Watchnian Shinjuku
  • GMT Shinjuku
  • Jack Road (within Nakano Broadway)

Route Details:

1. Walk from Hotel Gracery Shinjuku to Komehyo Shinjuku: Approx. 5-7 min walk. View on Google Maps

2. Walk from Komehyo Shinjuku to Daikokuya Shinjuku Main Store: Approx. 3-5 min walk. View on Google Maps

3. Walk from Daikokuya Shinjuku Main Store to Watchnian Shinjuku: Approx. 2-3 min walk. View on Google Maps

4. Walk from Watchnian Shinjuku to GMT Shinjuku: Approx. 1-2 min walk. View on Google Maps

5. From Shinjuku Station (near GMT) to Nakano Broadway (for Jack Road): Take the JR Chuo Line Rapid from Shinjuku Station to Nakano Station. Nakano Broadway is a short walk from Nakano Station. View on Google Maps (Approx. 15-20 min total travel time)

Route B: Handbag Focus

This route prioritizes pre-owned luxury handbags, starting in Shinjuku and then heading to Omotesando/Shibuya.

Top 5 Shops:

  • Komehyo Shinjuku Store
  • Daikokuya Shinjuku Main Store
  • Brand Off Shinjuku
  • Ginzo Shinjuku
  • Amore Vintage (Omotesando branch)

Route Details:

1. Walk from Hotel Gracery Shinjuku to Komehyo Shinjuku: Approx. 5-7 min walk. View on Google Maps

2. Walk from Komehyo Shinjuku to Daikokuya Shinjuku Main Store: Approx. 3-5 min walk. View on Google Maps

3. Walk from Daikokuya Shinjuku Main Store to Brand Off Shinjuku: Approx. 1-2 min walk. View on Google Maps

4. Walk from Brand Off Shinjuku to Ginzo Shinjuku: Approx. 5-7 min walk. View on Google Maps

5. From Shinjuku-sanchome Station (near Ginzo) to Amore Vintage (Omotesando): Take the Tokyo Metro Fukutoshin Line from Shinjuku-sanchome Station to Meiji-jingumae 'Harajuku' Station. Amore Vintage is a walk from there. View on Google Maps (Approx. 20-25 min total travel time)

Route C: Combined Focus

This route combines top watch and handbag shops, grouped by proximity.

Shops (Optimized Grouping):

  • Shinjuku Cluster: Komehyo Shinjuku Store, Daikokuya Shinjuku Main Store, Watchnian Shinjuku, GMT Shinjuku, Brand Off Shinjuku, Ginzo Shinjuku
  • Omotesando/Shibuya Cluster: Amore Vintage, Vintage Qoo, Atlantis Vintage
  • Nakano Cluster: Jack Road (within Nakano Broadway)

Route Details:

1. Shinjuku Loop (Walkable from Hotel Gracery): Visit Komehyo, Daikokuya, Watchnian, GMT, Brand Off, and Ginzo. These are all concentrated near Shinjuku Station. View Example Walk (Approx. 20-30 min walking for these shops.)

2. Travel Shinjuku to Omotesando/Shibuya: Take Tokyo Metro Fukutoshin Line from Shinjuku-sanchome Station to Meiji-jingumae 'Harajuku' Station. View Train Route (Approx. 10-15 min train)

3. Omotesando/Shibuya Loop (Walk): From Meiji-jingumae Station, walk to Amore Vintage, Vintage Qoo, and Atlantis Vintage. View Example Walk (Approx. 15-20 min walking for these shops.)

4. Travel Omotesando/Shibuya to Nakano: From Meiji-jingumae 'Harajuku' Station, take train (via Shinjuku) to Nakano Station, then walk to Nakano Broadway for Jack Road. View Train Route (Approx. 30-40 min total travel)
